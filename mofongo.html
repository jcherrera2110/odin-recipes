Fried plantain mashed with garlic, accompanied by onion, cheese, meat or pork rinds according to your taste

Mangu

    Ingredients

  • Plantains
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Garlic

    • For garnish

  • Cheese
  • Pork kind
  • Sancocho

To prepare the mofongo it is necessary to have a pestle to mash the plantains

    Steps to cook

  1. Peel the plantains into small slices
  2. Put oil to heat in a pan, to reach a temperature of 450c
  3. Pour the plantains into the hot oil
  4. Keep the plantains boiling at a temperature of 450c for 10 minutes
  5. Remove the plantains from the container and mash them with the pilon
  6. To compact them in the pilon, use butter and garlic while you remove it from the pan
  7. When the plantains is compact, take it out of the pilon with your hands and form a plantains ball
  8. Inside the ball add the ingredients of your choice, cheese or Pork Kind
  9. When you close the ball with your hands, put the ball in the pan again for 3 minutes
  10. When we take it out of the pan, drain the oil and it will be ready to eat
  11. You can accompany it with more cheese, pork rinds or sancocho broth
