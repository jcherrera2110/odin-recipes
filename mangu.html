Mashed boiled plantains, accompanied by onion, cheese, eggs or salami according to your taste

Mangu

    Ingredients

  • plantains
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter

    • For garnish

  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Salami
  • Onions

    Steps to cook

  1. Peel the plantains
  2. Bring water to boil in a pot
  3. Pour the plantains into the boiling water with salt to your liking
  4. Keep the plantains boiling at a temperature of 450c for 20 minutes
  5. Take the plantains out of the container and mash them with a fork until they are compact
  6. To compact them, use butter while you remove it from the container with boiling water
  7. With the fork, form a ball of mashed banana and accompany with the garnish of your choice
